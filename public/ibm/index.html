Bluemix Design Guide

Welcome!

The Bluemix Design Guide exists in order to build consistency across our product to create a unified experience for our users.

Our goal is to build and implement a Bluemix Design Guide based on a clear brand understanding. We envision our users being able to understand how to represent the brand of Bluemix and secure any needed element or asset in order to maintain consistency across any aspect of the product.

This is a living, learning design guide that is always evolving and growing with the help of our users.

Who we are

Bluemix is a robust cloud platform giving developers the confidence to build, deploy and scale cutting edge applications.

We are advocates for our user. We are a culture that embraces transparency, and believe by sharing code we can share knowledge.

Our users

We don't have just one. As Bluemix grows, more users emerge into our ecosphere. Each of them continue to challenge us to be technically sound and secure, as valued and required by our enterprise developers, and simple and intuitive, as necessitated by our Business Users.
